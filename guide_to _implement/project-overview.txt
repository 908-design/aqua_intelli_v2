## **Project Overview: AquaIntelli**

**What it is:** A groundwater intelligence platform that combines satellite data, AI forecasting, and government sensor networks to monitor and predict groundwater levels — essentially a "God's Eye View" of underground water resources.

---

## **Current Capabilities**

| Feature | What It Does | Data Source |
|---------|-------------|-------------|
| **GRACE Anomaly** | Measures changes in Earth's water mass from space | NASA GRACE-FO satellites |
| **Current Depth** | Real-time groundwater depth below ground | Central Ground Water Board (CGWB) |
| **Soil Moisture** | Surface soil wetness percentage | ESA Sentinel-1 radar |
| **Rainfall** | 180-day historical rainfall | NASA POWER |
| **30-Day Forecast** | AI-predicted groundwater depth in 30 days | Custom AI Model v2 |
| **90-Day Forecast** | AI-predicted groundwater depth in 90 days | Custom AI Model v2 |
| **Depletion Rate** | How fast water is dropping (meters/day) | Derived calculation |
| **Risk Assessment** | Warning/Critical/Recovering status | AquaIntelli engine |
| **Pipeline Mapping** | Underground water pipe depth visualization | Utility data |

**Coverage:** 847 zones, 10-meter resolution, currently tracking **Hyderabad, Andhra Pradesh** (17.385°N, 78.487°E)
